A bit about me.

I started coding when I was 10 years old doing game development with unity and c sharp. then after about 2 years I got the hang of it and could create mediocre games pretty easily.

Then when I was 12 I got into software engineering and web development, I watched a ton of videos and took courses online. At age 14 I completed many courses on backend web development and could create backend projects easily.

After that, I started delving into the world of free software and now hate apple and microsoft and love linux <3 (i use arch and vim btw)
